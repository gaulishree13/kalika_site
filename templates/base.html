Why Kalika?

Regardless of age, ethnicity, gender or family back- ground of our students, we support and help them achieve a balance lifestyle of physical, intellectual, emotional, social and spiritual well-being.

  • We offer career oriented courses in a reasonable cost, Kalika Public College is located at easily accessible place and out of the madding crowd of the city.
  • It has a rich library with sufficient text books, reference books, journals, business magazines with a well furnished, spacious and well ventilated reading rooms.
  • We offer well experienced and dedicated faculties and modern teaching techniques.
  • As per the syllabus demands, we send our students to different business organization, banks and finance companies for field reports and case studies.
  • Computer Lab, Play Ground, Canteen & Stage are available.
